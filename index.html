Why Silent Mind?

Because there is continuous chatter. The mind gets dispersed because of readily available digital distractions. This makes deep rest and creativity suffer, slowly at first, and over time creates a degenerative cycle of negative emotions and poor mental health, often physical also. It may be experienced as dullness, poor energy, lack of clarity, and disinterest.

What is Silent Mind

Silent mind is the cultivation of centeredness, improved reception (transistor analogy), and being at rest even while being in action. It needs conscious effort at first but can slowly become a state. It is experienced as in the form of better energy levels, lightness, harmony, and more frequent moments of inspiration.

Why do it in a group

People coming together has something magical to it. The common intentionality amplifies the journey for all individuals. Groups provide the opportunity to share experiences while pursuing a similar goal. This creates connection, provides support, and integrates new insights.
