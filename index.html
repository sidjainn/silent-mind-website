What is Silent Mind

Silent mind is the cultivation of centredness and improved reception. It requires practice at first and then becomes a natural state. Benefits include lightness, harmony, and more frequent moments of inspiration.

Why do it in a group

Shared intention amplifies the practice. Groups provide connection, support, and a place to integrate insights through sharing circles.
