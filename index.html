How it works

Observe. Remember. Brief. Draft.

01

Observe

It reads across the places your business actually happens — WhatsApp, Gmail, your calendar. Quietly, on your side.

02

Remember

Every commitment, payment promise and open thread lands in a memory that keeps your goals and priorities in view — and forgets nothing.

03

Brief

One short email, waiting when you wake up: today's three priorities, money outstanding, who's waiting, calendar traps. Two taps to say "done" or "not now" — it learns.

04

Draft

When something needs a reply, it writes one in your voice. You read it and press send. Nothing ever leaves in your name without you.

Plus a Friday punch list to close the week, and a Sunday check-in that keeps its memory sharp.

Privacy & control

Built to be trusted with the personal stuff.

It drafts — never sends

Anything outbound is a draft handed to you. Nothing leaves in your name without your thumb on the button.

Read-only where it counts

On WhatsApp it can read, but it cannot send — not "won't," can't. Sending is switched off at the system level, even if you ask.

Your memory is yours

Everything it remembers lives in plain files you can open, edit and take with you. No lock-in, ever.

A private vault

A gated notes file it never reads on its own and never quotes — opened only when you ask for it by name.

And honestly — what it doesn't do yet:

  • It reads text, not WhatsApp voice notes, images or attachments.
  • It watches one inbox, not several stitched together.
  • It works on the one computer it's set up on.

We'd rather tell you the edges up front than oversell you a demo.

Questions worth asking

Does it send messages or emails as me?

No. When something needs to go out, it writes a draft and hands it to you. You send it yourself — you stay in the loop on everything that leaves in your name.

Can it reply for me on WhatsApp?

No — WhatsApp is read-only. It reads your chats to track what matters, but sending is switched off at the system level, even if you ask. Want a reply? Ask it to draft one; you send.

Where does my data live?

What it reads stays on your machine and isn't used to train anything. The one exception is the brief email it composes, which is handed to a delivery service so it can reach your inbox — that's just how email works. Everything it remembers stays in files you own.

Do I need to be technical?

No. Setup is guided and we do the technical parts with you. You'll only handle the human steps — signing in to your accounts, approving a permission, scanning a WhatsApp QR code.

What do I need to run it?

A computer that stays awake during your day and a subscription to an AI assistant (Claude or Codex). We'll help you get set up during onboarding.

What if I stop?

You keep everything. Your memory lives in plain files you can open in any editor and take anywhere. Nothing is locked to us.
